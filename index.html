Why choose the WUST-GLOBAL?

Feature Benefits for You:

  • ACCSC Accredited: Our academic programs and degrees meet the high standards of academic quality set by the Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges (ACCSC).
  • Academic Focus: Learn the innovative subjects that provide career-focused skills you need for landing a job in today market, including STEM business administration, AI applications, cybersecurity, information technology, healthcare, and on.
  • Learn 100% Online: Take virtual interactive courses anywhere! Also, being online you will become part of the WUST community from day one and use full student services support, university facilities, libraries, and join student activities.
  • Affordable Tuition: Receive 50% discount on your regular tuition rate!

Online Study Overview

Our full-time intensive online courses help you develop career-focused skills using innovative technology-based approaches to enhance the academic and professional skills for success.

  • Course Length: 10-week virtual sessions with multiple start dates per year.

Why ACCSC Accreditation Matters?

The WUST-Global is a part of the Washington University of Science & Technology and is accredited by the Accrediting Commission of Career Schools and Colleges (ACCSC).

This means:

  • The academic programs and degrees have been rigorously reviewed and meet national standards of educational quality.
  • WUST-GLOBAL curriculum, faculty, and student services are focused on achieving outcomes that prepare you for your degree.
  • It ensures a commitment to continuous improvement and excellence in education.

Learn more about ACCSC.
